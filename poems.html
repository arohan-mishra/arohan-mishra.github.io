My Poems

Mother’s day

a story eighteen years and four months old
when i felt the warmth of your embrace in the bitter cold
i was one of the very lucky few
to have a great mother like you

you did so many things for me
that i couldn’t even see
you cooked and cleaned
worked day and night like a machine
so much and you never ever shunned
even when i pooped when i was one

for all the things you did
that no other parent does for their kid
i can never repay but can say
i love you mom and a happy mother’s day!

father’s day

Who is a father?
To a family what a pillar is to a mansion
Supporting our each and every action
A person who is always loving and kind
He always knows what we have in our minds

Who is a father?
A person who does all the hard work
Still sits away and let his family have the perks
Sometimes what he does is misunderstood
But in the end, it always lifts our livelihood

Who is a father?
A person who is always with you when you fall
Who helps you to stand again true and tall
For your every failure he hides his pain
But when you succeed, he is the first to open the champagne

Who is my father?
All of the above and an excellent pilot
Hearty and always ready to empty his wallet
To see us smile because of his big heart
All that he does, his love plays a big part
Such a person is my father!

Happy father’s day!!!
